To donate blood, please make sure

1. You are fit and healthy, and should not be suffering from transmittable diseases

2. You should weigh a minimum of 50 kg

3. You should be between 18 and 55 years of age

4. Your pulse rate is Between 50 and 100 without irregularities

5. Your Haemoglobin level is at a minimum of 12.5 g/dL

6. Your Blood pressure is within the range - Diastolic: 50–100 mm Hg, Systolic: 100–180 mm Hg

7. Your Body temperature is normal, with an oral temperature not exceeding 37.5°C

8. The time period between successive blood donations should be more than 3 months

9. You have not been tested HIV positive

10. Not suffering from cardiac arrest, hypertension, blood pressure, cancer, epilepsy, kidney ailments and diabetes

11. Not undergone ear/body piercing or tattoo in the past 6 months

12. You have not undergone immunization in the past 1 month

13. You are not treated for rabies or received Hepatitis B vaccine in the past 6 months

14. You have not consumed alcohol in the past 24 hours

15. You are not pregnant or breastfeeding, had miscarriage in the past 6 months

16. You have not undergone major dental procedures or general surgeries in the past 1 month

17. You did not have fits, tuberculosis, asthma and allergic disorders in the past
